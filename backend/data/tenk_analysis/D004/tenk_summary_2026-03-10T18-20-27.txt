10-K Summary: Clearwater Analytics Holdings, Inc.
Company: Clearwater Analytics Holdings, Inc. (CWAN)    Period: FY 2025
Period End: 12/31/25    Filing Date: 02/18/26
L1 — Headline
+ CWAN – Strong revenue growth amid pending $8.4B acquisition. | 02/18/26
L2 — Brief
CWAN reported 61.9% revenue growth to $731.4 million in 2025, driven by major acquisitions of Enfusion and Beacon Platform totaling $1.9 billion. The company agreed to be acquired by an investor group for $8.4 billion at $24.55 per share in December 2025, with closing expected in Q2 2026. Despite strong revenue and free cash flow growth, the company posted a net loss due to acquisition-related costs.

M&A & Deal Relevance
Pending Deals: Merger Agreement entered December 20, 2025 to be acquired by Investor Group for $8.4 billion at $24.55 per share
Deal Impact: Merger expected to close in second quarter of 2026 subject to stockholder approval and regulatory approvals. Share repurchase program suspended in November 2025 due to pending merger.
Strategic Alternatives: Not specified
Acquisition Activity: Completed acquisitions of Enfusion Inc. for $1.4 billion and Beacon Platform Inc. for $488 million in April 2025. Acquisition of Bistro asset from Blackstone completed March 31, 2025 for $112.7 million.
