Did financing certainty factor into board deliberations? Yes - as a positive factor for Boston Scientific
### Detailed Financing Analysis:
#### Boston Scientific Financing Profile:
Nature of Financing:
- No financing contingency: All Boston Scientific proposals explicitly stated the transaction would "not be subject to approval of Boston Scientific Stockholders or any financing contingency"
- This was highlighted in the very first written proposal on December 29, 2025 and remained consistent throughout
Financing Risk Characterization:
- Effectively zero financing risk - the absence of a financing contingency meant Boston Scientific was representing it had committed financing or balance sheet capacity to complete the transaction
- No financing due diligence by Penumbra was mentioned, suggesting Boston Scientific's financial capacity was not questioned
Consideration Structure:
- Mixed cash and stock consideration
- Final structure: ~73% cash (~$10.9B) and ~27% stock (~$4.0B) based on $374/share and implied equity value
- Cash portion required Boston Scientific to have or obtain approximately $10.9 billion in cash
- Stock portion required no financing as it involved issuance of Boston Scientific shares
Impact on Board Decision:
The absence of financing contingency was a significant positive factor contributing to "transaction certainty" - one of the Board's key selection criteria. This was particularly important because:
1. It eliminated a common source of deal failure
2. It demonstrated Boston Scientific's financial strength and commitment
3. It provided certainty of closing (subject only to regulatory approval and stockholder vote)
Comparative Analysis:
No other bidders participated, so no comparative financing analysis was conducted. However, the Board's repeated conclusion that financial sponsors were unlikely to make competitive proposals may have reflected concerns about:
- Financing contingencies typical in sponsor transactions
- Challenges in financing a transaction of this size (~$15B enterprise value)
- Current financing market conditions
Board Discussion:
While financing certainty was not extensively discussed in the excerpts (likely because it was not a concern), it was implicitly part of the "transaction certainty" factor that the Board repeatedly emphasized as important to their decision.
