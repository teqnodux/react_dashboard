"In November 2025, following an internal review of post-market data, the Company initiated a stop ship of its Brevera Breast Biopsy System Disposable 9 Gauge Needles (the "Brevera Needles"). The Company is continuing to conduct analysis regarding the potential impact of the current stop ship on Breast Health revenue, taking into account various potential mitigating measures, and the ultimate impact of the stop ship will be based on a number of contingencies, including some that are outside of the Company's control. However, in light of the Company's inability to presently predict the duration of the stop ship, the Company believes that it will be more challenging to achieve the 2026 milestone under the CVR agreement."
